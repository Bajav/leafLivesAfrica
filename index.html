    ABOUT US

    LEAF-LIVES AFRICA was founded by the end of 2021, inspired by the effects of COVID-19 that left many homes in despair. Our campaign contributes to climate change through clean air coalition and fighting hunger through sustainable agriculture.

    We believe that our environment serves us as we serve it in return, working tirelessly to preserve natural climates and atmospheres while ensuring food security for communities in need.
